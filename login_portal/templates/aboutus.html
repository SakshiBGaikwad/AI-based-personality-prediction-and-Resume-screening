About Us

PersoAnalyzer

The project aims to develop a prototype of a platform that aims to help the Users in identifying their personality traits. PersoAnalyzer seeks a more efficient way to analyse user's Resume and provide meaningful insights about suitable job field based on their Resume. PersoAnalyzer also aims to help an user to compare their Resume with the description of the job they are applying for and find a perfect match to kickstart thier career.

Our Mission

PersoAnalyzer will assist the user in better understanding themselves. PersoAnalyzer offers insights and analysis about the user's personality traits, appropriate career domain and identify a relevant fit for the job description. Users can make more optimized judgements about their skills, employment and personal development if they better understand themselves.
